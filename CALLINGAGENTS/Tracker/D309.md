# D309 — Knowledge Page Redesign

Full redesign of `/dashboard/knowledge` with 5-tier layout, Knowledge Health Score, drawer pattern for complex ops, granular chunk editing, and bulk AI answer generation.

## Scope
1. **Knowledge Health Score** — weighted formula (Completeness 35%, Coverage 25%, Freshness 20%, Sources 15%, Resolution 5%) with niche-specific targets
2. **Quick Add** promoted to Tier 1 — paste box + drawer triggers for Upload/Scrape/Compile
3. **Facts + FAQs inline editing** — per-item edit (✎) and delete (✕), collapsed FAQs
4. **Unanswered Questions** — paginated (7-10/page), checkboxes for bulk ops, "Bulk AI →" generates all in drawer
5. **Drawer pattern** for Upload/Scrape/Compile/Bulk AI — slide from right, maintains context, granular approve/edit/delete per extracted item
6. **Surface hidden components** — CallContextPreview ("Preview agent view"), GapAnswerSection (inline answer flow), TestQuerySection (= Ask Your Agent)
7. **Source provenance** — expand a source to see which chunks came from it
8. **Staleness indicators** — amber badges on facts >90 days old

## Key Decisions
- Drawer (not modal) for complex multi-step ops — Sonar-validated 2026-03-31
- Weighted health score with niche-specific targets
- Bulk AI: generate all selected in parallel → review per-item → approve
- Granular chunk control: individual approve/edit/delete on AI-extracted content
