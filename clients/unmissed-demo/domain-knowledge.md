## Niche Pain Points — Sales Reference

Use ONE line per call after identifying the caller's industry. Never stack them.

**Auto glass / windshield:**
- Shops miss 30–40% of inbound calls while technicians are on jobs.
- A missed same-day windshield replacement = $250–450 in lost revenue.
- Sensors and ADAS calibration upsells are missed when the caller gives up.

**HVAC:**
- Emergency service calls are $800–1,200 jobs. Miss the call, they Google the next HVAC shop in 10 seconds.
- After-hours calls in summer/winter are highest-intent — owner is asleep.
- Seasonal tune-up campaigns go to voicemail 40% of the time during busy season.

**Plumbing:**
- Flood/burst pipe = panic caller. High urgency = instant decision. Miss it and they're booked elsewhere.
- Weekend emergency calls have 2x the normal ticket value.
- Most plumbers miss 1–3 calls per day on job sites. At $300/call avg, that's $1k+ weekly.

**Barber / salon / hair:**
- No-shows cost $40–150 per slot. Automated reminder texts reduce no-shows by ~60%.
- Repeat booking rate goes up when clients can call to rebook after hours.
- New client calls from Google/Instagram go to voicemail if owner is cutting hair.

**Real estate (agent):**
- Buyer calls the day they're ready to buy. Hit voicemail = they call the next agent.
- Hasan Sharif (founder) is a realtor — this is how the product started.
- Seller lead calls from yard sign or Zillow are the highest-intent, shortest-window leads.

**Property management:**
- Tenant emergencies at 2am: flood, lockout, no heat. Miss it = liability + bad reviews.
- Leasing inquiry calls from ads go unanswered on weekends.
- One missed maintenance call that becomes a mold issue can cost $5–20k.

**Dental / medical clinic:**
- Patients in pain call 3 offices. First one to answer wins the booking.
- After-hours appointment inquiry = patient chooses whoever calls back first.
- Front desk is busy with patients — calls go to hold or voicemail.

**Legal:**
- Legal callers interview 3–4 firms before retaining. Pick up = 70% chance of retaining.
- PI / personal injury calls are time-sensitive — statute of limitations awareness creates urgency.
- High-value divorce/estate callers won't leave a voicemail for something that personal.

**Roofing:**
- Storm damage = 2-day window of high-intent calls. Miss the surge, miss the season.
- Large roofing jobs run $8,000–25,000. One missed call in storm season = major loss.
- Most roofers are on roofs during the day when phones ring most.

**Landscaping / lawn care:**
- Spring onboarding rush: one missed commercial quote call = $2–5k seasonal contract gone.
- Seasonal workers don't always answer client calls professionally.
- Existing clients who can't reach you will try a competitor at renewal.

**Restaurant / food service:**
- Missed catering inquiry = $500–5,000 event gone to the next restaurant on the list.
- Reservation calls during dinner service go unanswered — revenue lost.
- Corporate catering contracts start with a phone call. First to respond wins.

**General contractor / trades:**
- Missing a new customer call during a job site day = $2,000–10,000 project opportunity.
- Contractors who answer fastest win. Most don't answer at all.
- Referral calls from happy clients are highest-intent — they feel disrespected by voicemail.

## Features Detail

**IVR menu:** Optional press-1 / press-2 pre-filter before the AI agent answers. Most clients skip it — the AI handles routing naturally. Available on all plans.

**Live call transfer:** Agent detects a hot lead and transfers the call live to the owner's cell. If owner doesn't answer, agent takes a message and offers booking.

**Automatic SMS:** After every call, the caller gets a text with whatever action was taken (quote follow-up, booking confirmation, setup link, etc.). Configurable per niche.

**Calendar booking:** Integrates with Google Calendar. Agent checks availability, books appointments, sends confirmation texts — no human needed.

**Lead scoring:** Every call gets tagged HOT / WARM / COLD / JUNK. Owner sees the score instantly in the Telegram alert.

**The Learning Loop:** Weekly AI review of call patterns, missed questions, and gaps. Suggests prompt improvements. Keeps the agent getting smarter.
