## What "Polished" Means
The difference between auto_glass (polished) and restaurant (not polished):
- **Polished**: TRIAGE_DEEP has exact scripted responses, NLM-validated conviction variables, NICHE_EXAMPLES with 3-4 real scenarios, tested edge cases
- **Not polished**: Template defaults exist but generic, no NLM audit, might feel robotic on real calls
