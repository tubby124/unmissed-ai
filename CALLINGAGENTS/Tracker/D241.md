# D241 — Onboarding: Add Intent Question to Step 2/3

## Problem
Onboarding asks what services the business offers but never asks: "What do you most want callers to do?" Without this, the generated prompt has no primary intent — it defaults to info-bot.

## Action
Add one question to `step3-capabilities.tsx` or `step2-services.tsx`:
- "What's the #1 action you want callers to take?" → options: Book appointment / Get a quote / Transfer to staff / Leave info
- Store as `intake_data.primary_intent`
- Map to `call_handling_mode` or inject as PRIMARY GOAL in `buildPromptFromIntake()`

## Files
- `src/app/onboard/step3-capabilities.tsx`
- `src/lib/intake-transform.ts` — `toIntakePayload()`
- `src/lib/prompt-builder.ts` — `buildPromptFromIntake()`

## Acceptance
- New question visible in onboarding step 3
- `primary_intent` stored in `clients` or `intake_data`
- Generated prompt includes PURPOSE section above TRIAGE
