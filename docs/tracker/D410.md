# D410 — Agent Brain: proactive gap detection from call data

**Status:** 🟡 Backlog (Phase 5+)
**Priority:** LOW (not a Phase 1 PM blocker, but a key differentiator)
**Date:** 2026-04-05

## What
Analyze call transcripts to surface gaps in the agent's knowledge:
- "Your agent was asked about parking 14 times but has no parking info — add it now"
- "Tenants asked about rent balance 8 times — consider adding a billing FAQ"
- "3 callers were confused by your hours — do you want to update them?"

NLM research finding: "No competitor (Intercom, Ada, Voiceflow, Retell, Bland, Vapi) has a unified 'what your agent knows' dashboard. This is greenfield." — 2026-04-05 UI Alignment research

## Why It Matters
Right now a PM who buys unmissed has no way to improve Jade except re-reading call logs manually. Gap detection converts passive recordings into active product improvements. It's the feature that creates retention and expansion ("I added 6 new FAQs this week based on what tenants asked").

## Scope
1. Query: aggregate unanswered/deflected questions by topic across last N calls
2. Cluster similar questions (embedding similarity or keyword grouping)
3. Surface top 3 gaps as "improvement suggestions" on the dashboard
4. One-click "Add to agent" → opens KnowledgeEngineCard pre-filled with the suggestion
