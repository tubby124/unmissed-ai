# D373 — Onboarding quality floor: bootstrap from working-agent patterns

## Problem

After onboarding, new agents don't sound like Windshield Hub, Hasan, or Urban Vibe. They sound generic.

The working agents are good because they were hand-tuned over months — real TRIAGE_DEEP from actual call patterns, KB from real content, custom FAQs from real caller questions, edge cases from real failures.

Onboarding gives a one-shot Haiku baseline. There is no path from "just onboarded" → "sounds like a mature agent."

## Root Cause

`NICHE_DEFAULTS` are generic placeholders — not distilled from what actually works in production.
The intelligence seed (Haiku generates TRIAGE_DEEP, greeting, urgency) gives a plausible baseline but not a good one.

## Fix

1. **Extract patterns from Windshield Hub, Hasan, Urban Vibe** — pull their actual TRIAGE_DEEP, FAQ pairs, urgency words, edge cases into `NICHE_DEFAULTS` as the gold-standard baseline for each niche
2. **New clients in that niche start from the real thing**, not a Haiku guess
3. **Onboarding confirms/overrides** rather than building from scratch

## What "good" looks like per niche
- `auto_glass`: TRIAGE_DEEP asks chip vs replacement, insurance claim, mobile vs shop. Not "what can I help you with?"
- `real_estate`: TRIAGE_DEEP qualifies buyer vs seller, timeline, pre-approved, area. Not generic.
- `property_management`: knows the portfolio, handles maintenance requests, passes to owner.

## Files
- `src/lib/niche-defaults.ts` — update with production-quality defaults per niche
- `src/app/api/onboard/generate-agent-intelligence/route.ts` — use niche defaults as few-shot examples for Haiku
