# D318 — Step 3 bloat — trim to mode selection only

## Problem
Step 3 ("Agent mode") currently contains 10+ distinct inputs:
1. 4 agent mode cards — **keep**
2. Services chips (niche-adaptive) — move to step 5
3. FAQ text capture (`callerFaqText`) — move to step 5 / remove (duplicates D321)
4. 3 caller reason inputs — move to step 5
5. Urgency words input — move to dashboard
6. Price range input — move to dashboard (or step 5 optional)
7. Call forwarding toggle + number — **remove from onboarding** (dashboard settings only)
8. IVR toggle + prompt — **remove from onboarding** (dashboard settings only)

## Impact
Overwhelming for new users. The step is a wall of scrolling. Users skip fields or abandon. The whole point of onboarding is to get them to a working agent fast.

## Proposed Step 3 (after trim)
Just the 4 agent mode cards. That's it. One clear question: "What should your agent focus on?"

Everything else moves:
- Services + caller reasons + FAQ → Step 5 ("Teach your agent")
- Call forwarding + IVR → Dashboard settings (already have cards there)
- Urgency + price → Dashboard settings or Step 5 optional section

## Dependency
- D321 (FAQ dedup) should be done at same time
- D317 (niche-adaptive placeholders) applies to wherever caller reasons land

## Files
- `src/app/onboard/steps/step3-capabilities.tsx` — gut most of it
- `src/app/onboard/steps/step4.tsx` — receive services + reasons sections
- `src/app/onboard/config/steps.ts` — update canAdvance if needed

## Size
Large — significant restructure of step 3 and step 5.
