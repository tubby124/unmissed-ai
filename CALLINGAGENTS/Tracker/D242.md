# D242 — Haiku Intent Inference for niche='other'

## Problem
When a new client picks niche='other', `buildPromptFromIntake()` has no niche-specific TRIAGE or tool defaults. The agent is nearly useless out of the box.

## Action
After client sets `niche='other'`, run Haiku inference:
- Input: business name, services list, primary intent
- Output: closest mapped niche + suggested PRIMARY GOAL text
- Let owner confirm or override before provisioning

## Files
- `src/app/onboard/step1-business.tsx` or `step2-services.tsx`
- `src/lib/prompt-builder.ts`
- New: `src/lib/niche-inference.ts`

## Acceptance
- No client reaches provisioning with `niche='other'` and no inferred goal
- Suggested niche shown to owner with "Looks right?" confirm step
