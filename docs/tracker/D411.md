# D411 — Calendar auth for PM (live showing scheduler)

**Status:** 🟡 Backlog (Tier 2)
**Priority:** LOW (after D403–D408 are done)
**Date:** 2026-04-05

## What
Right now, Jade collects preferred showing times from prospects and sends them to the PM as a [SHOWING REQUEST] tag. The PM then manually calls back to confirm.

Full version: Jade cross-references the leasing agent's Google Calendar, offers real available slots, books the showing, sends SMS confirmation to the prospect.

NLM finding: "The AI must cross-reference the leasing agent's live calendar, propose available time slots, book the showing, and trigger SMS/email confirmations to reduce no-show rates."

## What Exists
- `booking_enabled` flag exists in the system
- Calendar auth + booking flow works for OTHER niches already
- Gap: no calendar auth flow specifically for PM clients (leasing agent calendar, not just "the owner")

## What's Needed
1. `booking_enabled=true` + calendar OAuth for leasing agent
2. PM-specific booking slot: "showing" type (not service appointment)
3. SMS confirmation template: "Your showing at [address] is confirmed for [time]. Reply CANCEL to reschedule."
4. Add booking card to PM setup checklist (D407) as optional step
