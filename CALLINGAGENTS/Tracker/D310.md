# D310 — Knowledge Health Score

Weighted knowledge health score displayed at the top of the Knowledge page.

## Formula
```
Score = (Completeness × 0.35) + (Coverage × 0.25) + (Freshness × 0.20) + (Sources × 0.15) + (Resolution × 0.05)
```

## Niche Targets
| Niche | Facts | FAQs | Sources |
|-------|-------|------|---------|
| auto_glass | 15 | 10 | 3 |
| plumbing | 12 | 8 | 3 |
| property_mgmt | 20 | 12 | 3 |
| real_estate | 25 | 15 | 4 |
| general | 10 | 5 | 2 |

## Display
- Badge: 72/100 with color (red <40, amber 40-70, green >70)
- Progress bar with label
- Next action CTA: "Answer 5 more questions to reach 85%"
- Breakdown on hover/click

## API
- New: `GET /api/dashboard/knowledge/health` → returns score + breakdown
- Or: compute client-side from existing home data (facts count, FAQ count, chunk count, gap count, sources)

- [ ] Score computes correctly per niche
