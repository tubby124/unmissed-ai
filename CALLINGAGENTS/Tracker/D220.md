# D220 — Lead Queue / Callback Tracking View

## Problem

Owner has to dig through call logs to find who to call back.

## Solution

- HOT/WARM leads sorted by recency, grouped by callback preference
- "Mark called back" action → sets lead_status='called_back'
- Count badge on home: "3 leads to follow up"
