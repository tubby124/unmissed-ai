# D416 — Tier restructure: move transfer to Core, clarify Lite as smart voicemail

**Status:** 🔴 Open
**Priority:** HIGH
**Date:** 2026-04-05

## Decision
Restructure the three tiers so Core is the obvious buy and Pro is just "more of the same at volume."

## New Tier Design

### Lite — Call Catcher ($49/mo)
Smart voicemail. You never have to open the app — everything lands in Telegram.
- Full triage conversation quality (same as all plans)
- Lead classification: HOT/WARM/COLD/JUNK (yes, Lite gets this — it IS the core value)
- Telegram + email summaries after every call
- SMS follow-up to caller
- 1 GBP scrape (agent knows the business basics)
- 100 min/month
- NO: multi-source knowledge, PDF upload, transfer, booking, coaching loop

### Core — AI Receptionist ($119/mo)
The full product. Agent knows your business, handles the call start to finish.
- Everything in Lite +
- 3 websites + 5 PDFs (agent is genuinely knowledgeable)
- **Transfer** ← moves from Pro to Core (key change — solves PM problem)
- Calendar booking
- Learning loop (weekly AI coaching)
- 400 min/month

### Pro — Front Desk Pro ($229/mo)
Core at volume for bigger teams.
- Everything in Core +
- 1,000 min/month
- 10 websites + 20 PDFs
- IVR pre-filter (multi-department routing)
- No new exclusive features — just scale

## Impact
- Resolves D412 (PM transfer gated to Pro)
- Makes Core the clear "default buy" for any business that wants an AI receptionist
- Lite is honestly positioned as smart voicemail — not a gimped agent
- Pro becomes a volume/infrastructure upgrade, not a feature unlock
