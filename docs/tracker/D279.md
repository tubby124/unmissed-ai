# D279 — Niche-Contextual Knowledge Editing

## Problem
Every niche has different knowledge types. A plumber needs: services, emergency words, pricing. A restaurant needs: menu, dietary restrictions, hours. A realtor needs: listings, open houses, market stats. But the dashboard shows the same generic "FAQs" and "Context Data" for everyone.

## Vision
Dashboard knowledge section adapts to niche:
- **Plumbing:** Services & Pricing | Emergency Dispatch Rules | Service Area
- **Restaurant:** Menu & Specials | Dietary/Allergy Info | Hours & Reservations
- **Real Estate:** Listings | Open Houses | Market Stats | Buyer vs Seller
- **Auto Glass:** Services & Pricing | Insurance/Claims Process | Mobile Service Area

Each section maps to template variables that flow into the prompt.
