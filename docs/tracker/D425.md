# D425 — Telegram onboarding: make notification setup a required onboarding step (not optional settings)

**Status:** 🔴 Open
**Priority:** HIGH
**Date:** 2026-04-05

## Problem
The entire "never need to open the app" value prop requires Telegram to be connected. But Telegram setup is currently a post-onboarding settings step — it's buried in the notifications settings tab.

Result: many trial users complete onboarding, make a test call, and never see a Telegram notification because they didn't know to set it up. They assume notifications are "just email" and dismiss the product.

For a PM specifically, Telegram IS the product. The [P1 URGENT] alert at 2am that says "Flooding — Unit 4A — John Smith (416-555-1234)" — that's the reason they pay. If they never connected Telegram, they never saw it.

## Solution
Move Telegram setup into the main onboarding flow — either as a dedicated step or as a required step on the trial welcome screen.

**Option A — Add as onboarding step 3 (between niche config and trial start)**
- "Connect Telegram to get call alerts" 
- Show the bot link, QR code, and the /start command
- "Skip for now" link — but default to requiring it

**Option B — Trial welcome gate**
- After onboarding completes, show a "Set up your notifications" screen before the dashboard loads
- Can't skip without explicitly choosing "I'll do this later"
- Show sample notification preview: what a [P1 URGENT] looks like in Telegram

**Option C — Onboarding completion checklist item**
- Add "Connect Telegram" as step 1 in D407's PM Setup Checklist
- Required before marking setup_complete = true

Recommendation: Option C is the fastest. Option B is the most effective (shows the value immediately).

## Why This Is a Priority
The "you never have to open the app" promise fails silently if Telegram isn't connected. A trial user who opens the app, sees some call logs, and doesn't understand why they're paying — they churn. A trial user who gets their first Telegram ping 10 minutes after onboarding — they understand the product immediately.
