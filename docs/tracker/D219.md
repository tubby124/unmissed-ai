# D219 — Missed Call Auto-SMS

## Problem
When a call is very short (<10s) or classified as JUNK, and the caller's phone is known,
there's no automatic follow-up. Caller just goes away.

## Solution
After call completion in /api/webhook/[slug]/completed:
- If duration < 10s OR classification='JUNK'
- AND caller phone is known
- THEN send: "We missed your call — call us back at [number]"
- Rate-limit: 1/phone/24h to prevent spam

## Relation
Different from D192 (fires on info-captured calls — already done).
This fires on SHORT/MISSED calls where nothing was captured.

## Connections
- → [[Features/SMS]] (requires sms_enabled + twilio_number)
