# D350 — Knowledge source drawers don't expand on click

## Bug
On the Knowledge page, the KNOWLEDGE SOURCES section shows 6 source cards (Website, Facts & Q&A, Text Imports, AI Compiler, Documents, Google Profile). Each has a `>` chevron suggesting they expand. But clicking them does nothing — no drawer, no list of chunks, no detail view.

## Observed (2026-04-01)
User uploaded a menu via AI Compiler → saw "12 chunks active" on the card → clicked the AI Compiler card → nothing happened. No way to see what 12 items were extracted or edit/delete individual items.

## Expected
Clicking a source card should expand to show:
- List of chunks from that source
- Each chunk: content preview, category badge (Pricing/FAQ/General), edit/delete actions
- Bulk actions: select all, delete selected, re-extract

## Impact
User has no visibility into what their agent actually learned. They uploaded a menu but can't see the individual items, verify accuracy, or fix mistakes. This breaks the "teach your agent" feedback loop.
