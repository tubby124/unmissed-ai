# D407 — PM Setup Checklist (QuickSetupStrip variant)

**Status:** 🔴 Open
**Priority:** MEDIUM
**Date:** 2026-04-05

## Problem
A property manager who logs into the dashboard for the first time sees 20+ settings cards with no guidance on what to do first. They won't know:
- They need to add an emergency tech number (`after_hours_emergency_phone`) or P1 calls can't transfer
- They should add maintenance contacts so Jade knows who to call
- Their tenant roster is live in `context_data`
- They should enable `after_hours_behavior: route_emergency` for 24/7 coverage

The existing `QuickSetupStrip` is generic. PM clients need a PM-specific version.

## Solution
Add a PM-specific setup checklist that renders at the top of settings when `niche === 'property_management'` and `setup_complete === false`.

Steps (ordered by impact):
1. ✅ Jade is live and answering calls
2. ⚠️ **Add emergency tech number** → enables P1 live transfer (links to forwarding_number field)
3. ⚠️ **Add maintenance contacts** → Jade knows who to dispatch (links to maintenanceContacts onboarding recap)
4. ℹ️ **Review tenant roster** → N tenants loaded (links to context_data / VIP contacts)
5. ⚠️ **Make a test P1 call** → say "my pipe just burst" and confirm transfer works

Each step has a status indicator. When all 5 are green, show "Your PM agent is fully configured" and hide the checklist.

## Files
- `src/components/dashboard/settings/SetupCard.tsx` or a new `PmSetupCard.tsx`
- `src/components/dashboard/settings/AgentTab.tsx` — render PmSetupCard at top when PM niche
