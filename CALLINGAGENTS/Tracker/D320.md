# D320 — urgencyWords not stored independently

## Resolution
Superseded — urgencyWords field replaced by intelligence seed `URGENCY_KEYWORDS` generated by Haiku in `/api/onboard/generate-agent-intelligence`. The seed generates niche-specific urgency triggers from GBP + business context and stores them in `niche_custom_variables.URGENCY_KEYWORDS`. No separate `urgency_words` column needed.

## Original Problem
`urgencyWords` was collected in step 3 UI but only survived if baked into TRIAGE_DEEP by the AI generation call. If the call failed, urgency words were silently lost.
