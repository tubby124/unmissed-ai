# D325 — Onboarding visual redesign

## Problem
Current onboarding UI looks dated:
- Dark theme with indigo-on-dark-purple looks generic SaaS
- Dashed borders, muted colors, low contrast cards
- No wow factor — first impression of the product

## Scope
Full visual pass on the 3-step onboarding flow (D323):
- Step 1: GBP search with niche badge, voice toggle
- Step 2: Pricing cards (match homepage or improve both)
- Step 3: Launch with orb animation

## Approach
Must go through `/ui-ux-pro-max` for design specs before implementation.

Consider:
- Light/dark mode that actually looks polished
- Pricing cards that match the homepage (or are even better)
- The orb as the visual anchor — consistent with dashboard
- Mobile-first (many business owners onboard from phone)
- Progress indicator that feels fast, not like a wall of steps

## Dependency
- D323 (3-step flow) — design the new flow, not the old 7-step one
- D324 (Plan = Mode) — pricing cards replace mode selection
