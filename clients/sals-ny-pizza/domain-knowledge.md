Dine-in, takeout, and delivery available. Known for massive 20-inch pies and garlic knots.

## Policies
- Delivery: within 8km of shop. $5 delivery fee. Minimum order $25.
- Delivery time: usually 30-45 minutes. Busy nights (Fri/Sat) can be up to 60 min.
- Payment: cash, credit/debit, Interac. No payment over the phone.
- Reservations: accepted for dine-in, groups of 6+
- Allergies: kitchen is NOT nut-free. Gluten-free crust available (+$3). Cannot guarantee cross-contamination.

## What Sal needs from the agent
1. Take phone orders: items, size, pickup vs delivery, name, phone, pickup time or delivery address
2. Answer menu questions: prices, ingredients, specials
3. Handle catering inquiries: date, guest count, budget, contact info → Sal calls back with quote
4. Take reservations for groups of 6+: date, time, party size, name, phone
5. Route complaints to Sal directly
6. Never promise exact delivery times — say "usually 30-45 minutes"
7. Never offer discounts or refunds without Sal
8. For allergies: always say "our kitchen is not nut-free and we can't guarantee no cross-contamination — please check with us in person if you have a severe allergy"
