# D388 — Complete Niche Questions: Services + Excluded + Emergency Definition

## Problem
Niche files are incomplete — they collect some info but are missing the 6 critical questions from NLM Q2 research. Specifically every niche is missing:
1. Services offered (as chips) — only auto-glass has this. Plumbing, HVAC, etc. don't.
2. Services NOT offered (negative constraints) — no niche collects this
3. Emergency definition — plumbing has yes/no/24-7 but not "what counts as P1"

## NLM Q2 — 6 must-ask questions for trade services:
1. What defines Priority 1 emergency? (specific triggers, not just yes/no)
2. What technical data must be collected before dispatch? (VIN, chip/crack, unit age, etc.)
3. Insurance / diagnostic fee handling?
4. Transfer + fallback protocols?
5. Geographic service area?
6. Services you do NOT handle? (negative constraints)
