# D273 — Pre-populate prompt filling from best available source

## Reframe (2026-03-31)
This is NOT "collect more onboarding data." It's:

**Pre-populate the filling variables from the best available source, then let the owner confirm or override.**

See [[Decisions/Prompt Sandwich Ownership Model]] for the full philosophy.

## The Three Onboarding Tiers

1. **"Decide for me"** — Pick niche, we pre-fill from NICHE_DEFAULTS + buildNicheFaqDefaults(). Click through. Done.
2. **"Let me tweak"** — Show pre-filled values, they edit what's wrong, confirm the rest. Their edits = authoritative.
3. **"Here's my stuff"** — Upload docs/website, AI extracts structured data, populates the same variables. Owner confirms.

## Variable Population Priority

1. Owner's explicit input (always wins)
2. AI-extracted from uploaded docs/website (D246 + AI compiler)
3. Haiku-inferred from business description (D247 TRIAGE_DEEP)
4. Niche defaults (our fallback)

## Questions to Capture per Tier

### Tier 1 (niche selection only)
- What's your business name?
- What niche/industry?
- Owner name?
→ Everything else from NICHE_DEFAULTS

### Tier 2 (tweak mode)
- "What questions do callers ask?" → FAQ_PAIRS, TRIAGE_DEEP
- "What services do you offer?" → SERVICES_OFFERED
- "What's your pricing approach?" → pricing_policy
- "Any services you DON'T offer?" → SERVICES_NOT_OFFERED
- Urgency signals → URGENCY_KEYWORDS
- Price range → context for triage

### Tier 3 (upload mode)
- Website URL → scrape → AI compiler → variables
- Pricing sheet upload → AI extract → FAQ_PAIRS + knowledge_chunks
- Business description → D247 Haiku → TRIAGE_DEEP
