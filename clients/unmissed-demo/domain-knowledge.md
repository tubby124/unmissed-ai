## What We Do

unmissed.ai is an AI voice receptionist platform for small businesses. We answer calls, qualify leads, and alert owners instantly — 24/7, flat rate.

## Pricing

- **$147/month** — flat rate, unlimited calls
- No per-minute charges. Same bill whether 10 or 1,000 calls.
- No contracts. Cancel anytime.
- Setup: self-serve at unmissed.ai/onboard (~5 min) or done-for-you in 24 hours

## How It Works

1. Business owner signs up and selects their niche
2. They forward their existing business number to us (standard carrier call forwarding — no app, no hardware)
3. When a caller calls, our AI agent answers, qualifies them, and alerts the owner
4. Owner gets a Telegram + SMS alert instantly. HOT leads can be transferred live.
