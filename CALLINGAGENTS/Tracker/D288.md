# D288 — Live Agent Capability Preview ("Your agent can do X right now")

## Problem
Users fill in settings and toggle features but have NO idea what their agent actually does. No surface says "right now, your agent can: take messages, answer 12 FAQ questions, send SMS follow-ups, and transfer urgent calls."

After onboarding, there's no "here's what you just built" moment. After changing settings, there's no "here's what changed about your agent" feedback.

## Vision

### Post-Onboarding Summary
Immediately after onboarding completes, show:
```
✅ Your agent is live! Here's what Alex can do:

📞 Answer calls 24/7 at (306) 555-0123
💬 Take caller name, phone, and what they need
📱 Send you a Telegram summary after every call
🔄 Transfer urgent calls to your cell

Your agent knows about:
• 12 FAQ answers about your services
• Your hours: Mon-Fri 8am-5pm
• Your emergency policy: business hours only

[Talk to your agent] [Go to dashboard]
```

### Dashboard Capability Card
On the overview page, always-visible:
```
What your agent does right now:
✅ Takes messages → Telegram alert
✅ Answers 12 common questions
✅ Sends SMS follow-up after calls
❌ Calendar booking (not set up yet) [Set up →]
❌ Call transfer (no forwarding number) [Add →]

Knowledge: 12 FAQ pairs, 3 business facts, website scraped
Last updated: 2 hours ago
```

### Settings Change Feedback
When you toggle a feature:
```
✅ SMS follow-up enabled! Your agent will now text callers
after collecting their info with a link to your business.
```

## Implementation
- Read from `buildCapabilityFlags()` + `knowledge_chunks` count + `extra_qa` count
- Map each capability to a human-readable description
- Show what's active, what's not, and how to activate missing capabilities
- Deep-link inactive capabilities to their setup card
